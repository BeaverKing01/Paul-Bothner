  • N1 City SuperStore

    Our N1 Branch is in the N1 Value Centre, adjacent to the N1 City Mall, with easy access off the N1 freeway. We offer a wide selection of industry standard products and brands to suit everyone’s pocket, tastes and requirements.

    We offer everything from vintage Fender guitars, to the latest in Studio and PA equipment. Come visit us and talk to one of our specialists about your needs. If it’s not present in-store, we’ll order and supply to you, quicker than waiting for an international online store. One of the largest music retail outlets in the Western Cape, much like our Plumstead Superstore, we’re focused on providing to a large market of needs, from entry-level to the most advanced.

    Our instruments are also of a superb quality, whether they’re budget or professional, something which the Paul Bothner reputation is based on. Our legacy of quality and low pricing is famous amongst our long-term customers who’ve grown accustomed to our attitude towards gear and customer service delivery.

    We take an ethos of excellent service and product knowledge super seriously! Our friendly, knowledgeable staff is at hand, to guide and advise you on the selection of the right equipment, to suit your particular needs and budget.

    Our branch is highly reputable and carries a massive range of equipment!! Be it heavy metal, or delicate classical music, we have the gear to suit your needs.

    The drum department has grown and carries all the brands you love. From the basic Drumlab kits to the more advanced Mapex, Tama and Yamaha kit, our drum department is loaded with some of the most popular and reliable gear around. Our Roland electronic kits offer incredibly accurate note tracking and acoustic simulation, considered to be of the highest quality available. Our range of electronic percussion devices is vast. Why not pop by to have a look at what these are capable of?

    We’re well recognized for our keyboard department. Our specialists will assist you, with gear ranging from full digital stage pianos, to small home keyboards. Our Hi-Tech and PA department has also grown extensively and offers everything for anyone, from home enthusiast, to professional. Our MIDI keyboard controllers are of the best around, some of the Roland Cakewalk models offering FPT (Fast Processing Technology) to minimize jitter and maximize efficiency in studio or live
