  • Claremont Branch

    Jerome Da Silva Gouveia, is not only the Claremont Branch Manager but also Regional Head of drums and percussion. He’s played drums in a number of bands including Supernature and SubVerS as well as sessions, including a drumming gig with international hit band, The Mission. Naturally, it boasts one of the largest drum & percussion areas in the entire country.

    As we are situated close to the Newlands Rugby and Cricket Stadia, we also get visits from our country’s Sports Stars and other celebrities. We’re seriously committed to serving everybody with the same excellence and enthusiasm, of course… Our expert staff-members cater to all, whether it’s assistance related to Hi-Tech Recording environments, or educational material for toddlers. Just about anyone, with musical needs, will feel at home with our highly trained, friendly, experienced staff. We offer a diverse range of gear, from entry level instruments, to leading brands such as Roland, Fender, Gibson, Yamaha, Warwick, Ibanez, Korg, SWR, Laney, Ovation, Taylor and more.

    Our Hi-Tech staff is well versed enough to offer truly superb and advanced service to musicians looking for the right gear, for the particular application they need. With years of experience in the studio and live performance realms, we’ve helped newbies and pros alike, all with the intention of satisfying our customers’ musical development. We’re the agents for a great many hi-tech products and we have the know-how to advise you in the context of recording for different genres of music, as well as a great range of instruments, advising on the right kind of microphone and the right kind of soundcard, for the right kind of outcome.

    As some might not know, we’ve moved from our premises in Werdmuller Centre to the Stadium on Main building, just down the road. Why not pop by and have a look at our new premises? New open plan floor space, with the same great gear and much much more!
